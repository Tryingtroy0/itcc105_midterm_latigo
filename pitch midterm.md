# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot — Project Pitch

## Problem Statement: Why is the old menu-based portal inefficient?

The existing ASSCAT student portal relies on a traditional menu-driven navigation system that forces students to dig through multiple layers of menus, dropdowns, and tabs just to complete simple tasks like checking their clearance status or viewing their class schedule. This creates several critical pain points:

- **Cognitive Overload**: Students must memorize where each feature is buried in the menu hierarchy instead of simply stating what they need.
- **Time Inefficiency**: A task as simple as checking enrollment status can require 4–6 clicks and multiple page loads.
- **Accessibility Barriers**: Non-tech-savvy students, especially freshmen, struggle to navigate a system designed around the developer's logic rather than the student's intent.
- **No Context Awareness**: The portal treats every session as brand new — it cannot remember that a student is in the middle of enrollment week and proactively surface relevant information.
- **Single-Device Limitation**: The portal is not optimized for mobile, yet most ASSCAT students primarily use smartphones.

The result is a portal that students avoid until absolutely necessary, leading to missed deadlines, incomplete clearances, and unnecessary trips to the registrar's office.

## Industry 5.0 Justification: AI Empowering Humans, Not Replacing Them

Industry 5.0 shifts the focus from pure automation back to **human-centricity** — using technology as a collaborative partner that amplifies human capability. The ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot embodies this philosophy in the following ways:

1. **Augmentation, Not Automation**: The AI does not replace the registrar, the SSC officers, or the faculty. Instead, it acts as a knowledgeable assistant that helps students reach the right human when needed. For complex cases (e.g., grade disputes, scholarship appeals), the AI escalates to the appropriate office rather than attempting to resolve it alone.

2. **Inclusive Design**: By accepting natural language and voice input, the Copilot removes the literacy and tech-skill barrier, making campus services equally accessible to all students regardless of their digital background.

3. **Data Sovereignty**: Student data (clearance records, grades, enrollment) remains within ASSCAT's own systems. The AI is a local decision layer — it reads and routes, but never stores sensitive data externally.

4. **Human Override Always Available**: Every AI response includes a clear path to a real human (e.g., "Talk to the Registrar"). The AI empowers choice; it never creates dependency.

## Agile Requirements: User Stories

### User Story 1 — Clearance Check
> **As a graduating student**, I want to ask the AI "Am I cleared for graduation?" in plain language, **so that** I can instantly know which offices still have holds on my record without navigating through multiple portal tabs.

### User Story 2 — Schedule Assistant
> **As an enrolled student**, I want the AI to show me my class schedule and notify me of room or time changes, **so that** I never miss a class due to a last-minute update I would have otherwise missed on the bulletin board.

### User Story 3 — Offline Emergency Mode
> **As a student with limited internet connectivity**, I want the AI Copilot to function in an offline mode that still provides cached essential information (like my last known schedule and clearance status), **so that** I can access critical campus information even when mobile data is unavailable.
